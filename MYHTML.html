Internship Details

Internship Title: Digital Literacy & ICT for Rural Development

Location: Chinakakani Village, Guntur District

Duration: May 11, 2025 – June 10, 2025

Activities Conducted

  • Taught villagers how to use mobile phones for basic functions like saving contacts and using UPI apps.

  • Conducted geotagging activities and submitted reports daily.
  • Organized group yoga and meditation sessions for community well-being.
  • Provided personal assistance in solving mobile-related issues.

My Learnings

This internship helped me improve my communication skills, leadership abilities, and practical knowledge about rural digital challenges. I also learned how to work with different people and solve real-life problems effectively.
